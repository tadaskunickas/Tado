These are some interesting podcasts and websites that have provided me value. I hope you gain something out of them!

Joe Rogan and Dr Rhonda Patrick

This Joe Rogan podcast with Dr Rhonda Patrick is about holistic health; diet, exercise, ways of life.

Joe Rogan and Dr Andrew Weil

Dr Andrew Weil is well known physician and author. This podcast focuses on the mind and body relationship, integrated medicine, and mental health.

NootropicGeek

Nootropics are supplements and vitamins for better brain health, focus, memory. This is a cool website on nootropics and their potential value.

Joe Rogan and Elon Musk

I'm a big fan of Elon Musk's technology and I think it's interesting to hear him speak unscripted.

Think fast, speak smart

This is a great podcast from the Stanford School of Business on effective public and spontaneous speaking. Watch this if you get nervous before public speaking!
